DeaSan! 

        Experience the beauty of Deasan (دیسان), situated approximately 2,200 meters above sea level. This stunning location is renowned for its breathtaking scenery and natural charm. It takes about 2 hours to reach here by foot from Atrood via the Samarbagh route, while traveling by car through the White Stream route takes just one hour. As you walk through the flowing rivers and lush valleys, you'll truly feel the splendor of nature. This place promises to provide an unforgettable experience!
